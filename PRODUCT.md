# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Personal finance trackers who want a highly secure, private, offline-first dashboard to monitor net worth, investment performance, loans, insurance policies, and passwords. They need clarity, readability, and confidence without cloud storage or tracking.

## Product Purpose
To serve as a fast, offline-first personal financial command center. Success means presenting a comprehensive, type-safe, and visually clear summary of the user's financial health, asset growth, and goal alignment without clutter.

## Brand Personality
- Trustworthy
- Snappy
- Minimalist

## Anti-references
- "SaaS-slop" card grids with gradients, arbitrary shapes, and tiny labels.
- Slow, choreographed screen transitions or page loaders.
- White card backgrounds that clash in dark mode.

## Design Principles
1. **Performance First**: Transitions should be snappy (<250ms) and use hardware-accelerated properties (transform, opacity).
2. **Restrained Accentuation**: Accent colors are saved for interactive triggers (primary actions, active tabs) and critical state changes.
3. **Structured Density**: Pack dense financial information cleanly with consistent typography, tabular numbers, and generous but structured margins.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratio ≥4.5:1 for body and data labels.
- Tabular figures for numerical data to ensure column alignment.
- snappier, non-distracting layouts.
